The report is divided into two main dashboards:

1. Credit Card Customer Report
This view provides a deep dive into the customer base, segmenting metrics by demographics.

Key Metrics: Total Revenue, Total Interest, Total Income, Customer Satisfaction Score (CSS).

Visualizations:

Revenue by Age Group, Marital Status, and Income Group.

Revenue vs. Gender (Line Chart).

2. Credit Card Transaction Report
This view focuses on financial performance and transaction behaviors.

Key Metrics: Transaction Count, Transaction Amount, Annual Fees.

Visualizations:

Quarterly Revenue & Transaction Count trends.

Revenue by Expenditure Type (Bills, Entertainment, Fuel, etc.).

Revenue by Card Category (Blue, Silver, Gold, Platinum).

Transaction Method analysis (Swipe vs. Chip vs. Online).

Based on the data analysis, the following actions are recommended:

Targeted Marketing: Focus marketing campaigns in TX, NY, and CA as they yield the highest return.

Product Focus: Since "Bills" and "Entertainment" are the highest expenditure types, introduce rewards or cash-back offers in these categories to drive usage.
